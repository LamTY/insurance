Last Updated: May 15, 2026

CoverPro Insights ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website coverpro-insights.com (the "Site").

Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the Site.

When you visit the Site, certain information is collected automatically, including:

  • Log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited, time and date of visit, referring URLs)

If you contact us through our contact form, we collect the information you submit, including your name and email address.

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our Site. Specifically:

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect Site functionality.

This Site participates in the Google AdSense program. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our Site. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our Site and other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

We use the information we collect to:
