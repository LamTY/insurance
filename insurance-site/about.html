Our Story

About CoverPro Insights

We exist because professionals deserve insurance information written specifically for their situation — not generic advice recycled from financial textbooks.

Our Mission

CoverPro Insights was founded with a single purpose: to create genuinely useful insurance content for professionals who face questions that generic guides never answer. A commercial airline pilot, a practicing physician, and a solo-practice attorney all have fundamentally different insurance needs. We address each one specifically.

Too much insurance content online is written to rank for keywords rather than to actually help readers. We take a different approach: we research each topic thoroughly, gather real quotes from multiple carriers, and write for a professional audience that can handle nuance and detail.

Our Editorial Standards

Every article published on CoverPro Insights meets the following standards:

  • Research-backed: Core claims are supported by insurance industry data, carrier documentation, or interviews with licensed professionals.
  • Profession-specific: We don't publish generic insurance advice. Every piece is tailored to the specific professional category it addresses.
  • Transparent about limitations: We are not licensed insurance agents or financial advisors. We clearly disclose this and recommend readers consult licensed professionals before making coverage decisions.
  • Regularly updated: Insurance rates and regulations change. We update our core guides when significant changes occur in the market.
  • Disclosure of commercial relationships: Where we earn commissions through affiliate relationships, we disclose this clearly in the article.

Our Coverage Areas

We publish in-depth guides for five professional categories:

  • ✈️ Pilot Insurance: Life insurance, disability coverage, and income protection for commercial airline pilots, private pilots, and aviation professionals.
  • 🩺 Physician Insurance: Life insurance, disability insurance, and malpractice coverage for physicians, surgeons, residents, and medical professionals at all career stages.
  • ⚖️ Attorney Insurance: Life insurance, professional liability (E&O), and disability coverage for attorneys in private practice, law firms, and public sector roles.
  • 🔨 Contractor Insurance: General liability, disability income, and life insurance for self-employed contractors, tradespeople, and small construction businesses.
  • 🌿 Senior Insurance: Final expense, Medicare supplement, long-term care, and life insurance options for adults 55 and older.

Important Disclosures

CoverPro Insights is an independent research and publishing website. We are not a licensed insurance agency, and we do not sell insurance products directly. Our content is for informational purposes only.

Some articles on this site contain affiliate links to insurance carriers or brokers. If you click through and purchase a policy, we may receive a referral commission. This does not affect our editorial recommendations — we only link to companies we believe provide genuine value based on our research.

All insurance rates referenced in our articles are estimates based on publicly available data and industry research. Actual premiums will vary based on your specific health history, location, coverage amount, and other factors. Always obtain actual quotes from licensed insurance professionals before making a purchasing decision.
